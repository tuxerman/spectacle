About

What

Spectacle intends to permanently archive, and enable citizens quickly search over large volumes of publicly-released government documents.

Of particular interest and inspiration are the thousands of documents released by the Government of India under the Right to Information Act, 2005.

Why

The RTI act is a powerful enabler. However, for all the effort spent (by both the citizens as well as the officials concerned) in retrieving this information, there is no single repository for it all. Spectacle aims to be this library, saving both public spirited citizens' time as well as that of the government by eliminating the need to make repeated queries for information that already exists somewhere.
